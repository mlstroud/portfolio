About

Originally from Texas, I moved to Washington in 2013. At first I lived in Bellingham for several years before moving to Woodinville, and finally to Kirkland where I currently live.

I've been in love with technology since 7th grade, where I first encountered HTML. I would spend countless hours making websites for my favorite games, books, and anything else I could think of.

When I was in 11th grade I began learning PHP from a friend who ran a website for a gaming community we were a part of. Once I graduated and began college, I was excited to major in Computer Science and begin my programming journey.

Gaming has been one of my passions for a long time, and is how I still remain in touch with many of my friends. I primarily enjoy RPGs, FPS, and strategy games.

Music is also a big part of my life. I grew up in a family of musicians and have played guitar for 17 years. In the future, I would like to devote the time to learn piano as well.

Although I was never much of a traveler, after visiting Hawaii both my wife and I have decided to change that. We started saving money and making a bucket list of places to visit including Japan, South Korea, and Paris.
